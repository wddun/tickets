Privacy Policy

WTS Tickets  ·  Last updated: March 28, 2026

WTS Tickets ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Will's Tech Support. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an organizer account, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
  • Event and attendee data: Events you create, including attendee names, email addresses, and ticket check-in status.
  • Camera access: The app uses the camera only to scan QR codes. We do not capture or store photos or videos.
  • Usage data: Standard server logs including IP addresses and request timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the WTS Tickets service.
  • To send ticket QR codes to attendees on your behalf.
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. Session data is stored server-side and expires automatically.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Amazon Web Services (AWS SES) to send transactional emails (ticket delivery). AWS may process recipient email addresses in order to deliver those emails. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from the dashboard (Settings → Delete Account). Deleting your account permanently removes all associated events, tickets, and personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

WTS Tickets is intended for use by event organizers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, use the in-app account management features or contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions: support@willstechsupport.com

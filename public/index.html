Features
Everything you need to run a great event

Designed to be simple for organizers and seamless for attendees.

🍎
Apple Wallet Passes
Attendees add their ticket directly to Apple Wallet. Passes update automatically when anything changes.
📷
QR Code Check-In
Scan tickets right from any phone's camera. Works offline-friendly and handles hundreds of guests.
📧
Automated Emails
Ticket confirmation and 24-hour reminder emails are sent automatically. Includes open tracking.
📊
Real-Time Dashboard
See who's checked in, who has their ticket, and who's opened their email — all live.
🔁
Multi-Entry Support
Enable re-entry tracking for events where guests come and go — like conferences or multi-day events.
👥
Team Access
Share event access with volunteers and staff at view-only or full-access levels.

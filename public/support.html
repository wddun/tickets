Privacy Policy

WTS Tickets ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Will's Tech Support. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and a hashed password.
  • Event and attendee data: Events you create, attendee names, email addresses, and check-in status.
  • Camera access: Used only to scan QR codes. We do not capture or store photos or videos.
  • Usage data: Standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) used solely for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the WTS Tickets service.
  • To send ticket QR codes to attendees on your behalf.
  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Amazon Web Services (AWS SES) to send transactional emails. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete it at any time from the dashboard under Settings.

6. Contact

For privacy questions: support@willstechsupport.com
